SIP Lab

Social &
Intergroup
Perception Lab

The SIP lab is a research lab composed of young researchers who are dedicated to studying how humans perceive others - whether individuals or entire groups - and how these perceptions are influenced by various social psychological factors. Currently, the SIP Lab focuses its research on attributions of humanity, both at the interpersonal and intergroup levels. The main research themes revolve around dehumanizing processes, such as the objectification of others, and humanizing processes, such as the anthropomorphism of digital technologies and pets.
